10 Interesting facts about Alaska

  1. Alaska's nickname is the last frontier
  2. It is home to 3 million lakes and 3,000 rivers!
  3. It's home to the tallest mountain in the world, from base to peak!
    (don't believe me? click here)
  4. And has more coastline than the rest of the U.S. combined!
  5. Alaska has an estimated 100,000 glaciers that make up about 5% of Alaska
  6. The Northern Lights can be seen on average more than 200 days per year
  7. Has one of the world's largest tides, which rises 35 feet
  8. The lowest temperature ever recorded was -80 degrees and the highest was 100 degrees
  9. Curt Schilling, Jewel, Carlos Boozer, and actress Holly Madison were raised in Alaska
  10. 98% of America's brown bears live in Alaska
