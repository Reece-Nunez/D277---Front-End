Kenai - The Heart of Alaskan Adventure

If you ever find yourself in the wilds of Alaska, there is definitely one place you would want to visit.
That place is Kenai. Kenai is home of the world-famous Kenai river; where the salmon fishing is world-class.
It is the ideal location for natural recreation, hunting, and all things wildlife.
Below you will find some basic information on this beautiful city.

  • Population: 7,838
  • Year incorporated: 1960
  • Region: Southcentral Alaska
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $82,085
  • Alaska's Average Income: $103,258
